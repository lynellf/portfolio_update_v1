Deck Optimizer (Spreadsheet)

This tool has allowed hundreds of Yu-Gi-Oh! duelists to build competitive decks with mathematial accuracy.

Players are able to configure their decks to maximize the probabilities of desired opening hands, while minimizing drawbacks. This project has spawned my desire to further improve my web development skills, as many duelists may not have access to a spreadsheet tool, or know how to operate it.
